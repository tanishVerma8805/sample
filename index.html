HOW TO USE THIS INVENTION

The use of the invention is explained stepwise step: -

  • Firstly the Rescue robot would be send inside the borewell through manly pulley via help of steel ropes to prevent breakage of ropes at any undecided case.
  • Two way communication system would come in action, via camera and strong bright torch we'll locate the trapped person's position, then via microphone and transmitter we'll try to contact the person if conscious or not.
  • If found unconscious then we'll provide water supply from the surface via pipe.
  • Meanwhile Temperature sensor and gas sensor would provide exact readings of the atmospheric conditions inside the borewell to the rescue team.
  • Automatic hand gesture control robotic arms would come in action, through camera, the arms (operated by the team through hand gesture control system), would hold the trapped person safely and pull it towards the robots base.
  • Pulse oximeter incribed on the palm of the robotic hand would regularly check and provide the readings of oxygen saturation levels of the person to the team.
  • If found low oxygen levels, then we'll provide oxygen from the surface via pipe.
  • The final step, Telescopic gear mechanism, after grabing the person the telescopic mechanism would expand downwards (in a straight line) automatically until it reaches down to provide a base to the person for ensuring extraa safety.
  • In continuation to the 8th point, a rubber balloon attached to the telescopic mechanism would inflate down to provide base to the person for stablization.
  • 10. The balloon would be filled with compressed air via pneumatic pipe given from the surface that goes through the telescopic gear mechanism (Note: this mechanism is attached to the robots outframe and is hollow from inside for passage of pneumatic pipe.)
  • 11. After analyzing each procedure step-by-step, it's time to pull the robot back towards the surface with the trapped person, via manly pulley.
